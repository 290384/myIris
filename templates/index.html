Iris Species Classification

Iris is a family of flowers containing several species such as the iris.versicolor, iris.setosa, and iris.virginica. The differnt species can be classified considering length and width of petals and sepals.
Petals & Sepals for Iris setosa, Iris versicolor, and Iris virginica (Sources: 1, 2, 3, Licenses: Public Domain, CC BY-SA 3.0 & CC BY-SA 2.0)
This site will help you to identify the correct species. The only thing you need to do is to enter length and width of petals and sepals.
